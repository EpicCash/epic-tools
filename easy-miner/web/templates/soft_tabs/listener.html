HTTP/S Listener is used to receive network transactions to your local wallet. Must be working and connected to running node when mining or receiving transactions from others.

- your port is open and reachable for others

- your port is closed in modem/router or system firewall is blocking it, receiving transactions via HTTP/S is impossible.

To solve this problem you can open your listener port or generate Ngrok address in Wallet App.
